Guest Speakers

Voices that ignite imagination and passion for the arts.

Mr. Himanshu Agrawal

Origami Artist

Himanshu Agrawal is an acclaimed origami artist from Mumbai, India, celebrated for transforming the ancient art of paper folding into breathtaking large-scale sculptures. With over three decades of dedication to the craft, he has redefined origami through innovation, scale, and storytelling. As the founder of Orukami, Himanshu has created monumental paper installations that merge precision, emotion, and design. His works—some extending over a hundred feet—have earned him multiple recognitions in the Limca Book of Records and global acclaim for their originality and technical mastery. Each piece reflects his belief that paper, though fragile, holds the strength to inspire awe and imagination. Through his art, Himanshu continues to bridge tradition with contemporary creativity, leaving an indelible mark on India's modern art landscape.

Ms. Kanika Singh

Mosaic Artist

Kanika Singh is a Delhi-based mosaic artist with over a decade of experience in transforming fragments of glass and tile into timeless works of art. What began as a small rooftop studio has grown into a thriving creative space, driven by her endless passion and dedication to the craft. Kanika's intricate mosaics reflect her deep respect for detail, colour, and meaning—each piece a celebration of patience, beauty, and artistic vision.
