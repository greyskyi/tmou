Competitions

We're hosting two exciting competitions, rangoli making and mosaic making, to spark creativity and vibrant expression.

Art Exhibition

The Art Exhibition showcases a stunning collection of art pieces created by artists of all ages. Each work reflects a unique perspective, blending creativity, emotion, and technique. This exhibition celebrates artistic expression in all its forms, inviting visitors to explore the imagination and stories behind every creation.

Workshops

We're hosting five live workshops designed to inspire creativity and skill. Participants can explore calligraphy, pottery, mug painting, cupcake decorating, and candle making, each offering a hands-on artistic experience.
