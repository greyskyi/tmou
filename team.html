A Message from Our Founder & CEO

'The Mosaic of Us' stands as a testament to the creative spirit, collaboration and vision of our students. Each artwork reflects an individual voice, yet together, they form a collective narrative - one that celebrates the richness of diversity and the power of unity. This student-led exhibition embodies the essence of our school's philosophy, empowering learners to lead with imagination, purpose and empathy.

Through art, our students remind us that innovation begins when curiosity meets courage and expression becomes a bridge between minds and hearts. I commend Nandini Mehrotra and every student who has contributed to this initiative- for their artistry, their leadership and their belief in the transformative power of creativity.

May 'The Mosaic of Us' continue to inspire all who witness it, reminding us that education, at its best, nurtures both intellect and soul.

Warm Regards,

Priti Agarwal

CEO & Founder
Billabong High International School, Kanpur

A Message From Our Event Director

Welcome to The Mosaic of Us. This exhibition proudly presents the creative achievements of all our artists, showcasing a diverse collection of artworks that reflect their unique perspectives and talents. Each piece contributes to a vibrant narrative celebrating individuality and the collective spirit within our school.

This event highlights the importance of artistic expression as a powerful form of education and connection. We invite you to engage with the stories and emotions conveyed throughout the exhibition, honoring the dedication and creativity of every participant.

Thank you for joining us in this celebration of art and community.

Warm Regards,

Nandini Mehrotra

Event Director
