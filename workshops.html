Live Art Workshops - Pottery, Cupcake Crafting & More | The Mosaic of Us

Live Workshops

Engage, create, and learn through hands-on artistic experiences.

Pottery

Experience the grounding art of pottery as you shape clay into expressive forms. This workshop invites you to connect creativity with touch, crafting pieces that reflect patience, precision, and individuality.

Cupcake Crafting

Explore the artistry of confectionery design by blending color, texture, and flavor. This workshop transforms baking into a creative expression where every cupcake becomes a canvas of imagination.

Mug Painting

Transform a simple mug into a personal work of art. Through guided techniques and your own inspiration, discover the joy of combining color, form, and function in everyday design.

Calligraphy

Immerse yourself in the meditative art of calligraphy, where every stroke embodies grace, rhythm, and intention. Discover how the flow of ink transforms words into timeless visual poetry.

Candle Making

Delve into the sensory craft of candle making, where fragrance and form unite. Learn the delicate balance of scent, wax, and color to create candles that radiate warmth and artistry.

All workshops taking place at the venue on the event day will be paid experiences, offering participants hands-on learning and creative engagement.
